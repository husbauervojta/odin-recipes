Lasagne

Description

It is slowly baked in the oven until the pasta is tender and the cheese on top becomes golden and crispy.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Onion and garlic
  • Béchamel
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. The meat sauce: Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Tomatoes: Stir in the tomato sauce and herbs, then simmer for 20 minutes on low heat.
  3. The béchamel: Melt butter in a pan, stir in flour to make a roux, and gradually whisk in milk until thick.
  4. The lasagna: Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish, add pasta sheets, and repeat layers with béchamel and cheese.
  5. Cook: in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 30–40 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Rest: the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing to keep the layers intact
