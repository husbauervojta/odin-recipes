Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is the ultimate "pantry meal" made with just a few basic staples. It features a simple but flavorful sauce of toasted garlic and olive oil, often with a hint of chili for heat.

Ingrediens

  • Spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Onion and garlic
  • Fresh parsley
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta:oil spaghetti in salted water until perfectly tender.
  2. Infuse oil:Gently heat olive oil in a large pan and add garlic slices until they turn light golden..
  3. Add heat:Stir in the red chili flakes for about 30 seconds to release their spice.
  4. Toss:Add the cooked pasta and chopped parsley directly into the oil and garlic.
  5. Finish: Squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the top and serve immediately.
